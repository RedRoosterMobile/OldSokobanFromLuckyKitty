Lucky Kitty

Lucky Kitty is meant to cheer you up. In "Settings" you can choose between up to 8 cute kittycats and 8 splendorous backgrounds if you treat your kitty well. Under "Awards" you can see your progress. If you tap your kitty 8 times a day you will be rewarded with lucky charm, new cats and new backgrounds. Furthermore you'll be able to play with your cat in three cute little games. The "Cute Talk" menu will seriously cheer you up and make you smile with the help of wise and charming quotes. Lastly the "Lucky Nut" is a classical Fortune Cookie but in a carefully selected witty way. Both, quotes and nuts, can be posted on Facebook. A cat will appear that repeats your current Lucky Nut or Cute talk line to share it with your friends. This will cheer them up too!

Have fun with your Lucky Kittys!
